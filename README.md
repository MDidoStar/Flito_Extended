---

## 🚀 What FLITO Can Do

### 🗺️ Interactive Map
Never get lost again. Explore any destination on a live, interactive map — search landmarks, detect your exact location, and discover what's around you in seconds.

### 🏨 Smart Hotel Finder
Tell FLITO your budget and preferences. It'll surface the best hotels with prices, facilities, and contact details — no scrolling through endless booking sites required.

### 🍝 Food Discovery
From hidden local gems to top-rated restaurants, FLITO finds the perfect dining spot for your taste and budget. Filter by cuisine, price, and rating — eat like a local, everywhere.

### 🏝️ Tourism & Activities
Museums, beaches, historical landmarks, adventure spots — FLITO curates the best experiences at your destination so you never miss what matters.

### 🚗 Transportation Guide
Getting around made easy. Compare taxis, buses, trains, flights, and car rentals — just enter where you're going and FLITO handles the rest.

### 🛍️ Shopping Companion
From luxury malls to local markets, FLITO helps you find exactly what you're looking for — wherever you are in the world.

### 💰 Real-Time Budget Tracker
Set your trip budget, log expenses on the go, and always know how much you have left. No surprises. No overspending.

### 💱 Live Currency Converter
Instant conversions between hundreds of global currencies with live exchange rates. Know exactly what you're spending, in any currency.

### 🗣️ Instant Translation
Communicate confidently in 50+ languages. FLITO gives you translations *and* pronunciations — so you can connect with locals, not just read signs.

### 📱 Phone Validation
Not sure if a number is real? Enter any phone number and FLITO will instantly 
verify if it's valid, identify its country of origin, and break down its 
format — so you always know who you're calling before you dial.


### ✈️ Trip Builder *(Premium)*
The crown jewel of FLITO. Generate a complete, personalized day-by-day itinerary — hotels, restaurants, activities, transport, and shopping — all tailored to your dates, budget, and travel style. Export it as a PDF and you're ready to go.
